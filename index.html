Controls:
Press the 'w' or 'up arrow' key to move the paddle up. Press the 's' key or 'down arrow' key to move the paddle down.
Press the 'r' key to restart.
Press the 'escape' button to pause and unpause the game. Note that you may need to hold down the escape key for a second for the change to take effect.
